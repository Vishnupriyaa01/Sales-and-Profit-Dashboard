                                                          SALES AND PROFIT DASHBOARD

Overview

This project shows the sales and profit performance of a retail company using Excel.The dashboard helps understand which products, customers, and states bring the most sales and profit.

Dataset

The dataset includes:
Order Date – date when the order was placed
State – location where the product was sold
Category – main product group (e.g., Furniture, Office Supplies, Technology)
Sub-Category – specific product type (e.g., Chairs, Phones, Binders)
Product Name – Name of the specific product sold
Sales – total money received from the sale
Quantity – number of items sold
Profit – actual earnings after deducting costs

Observation

The dashboard shows that Phones and Chairs are the top-selling and most profitable categories, while products like Paper and Fasteners have lower sales. Profit has generally increased over
the years, showing positive business growth. The number of customers has also grown each year, which means the company is expanding its reach. Sales are higher in some states, while others
have lower performance, indicating areas where the company can focus more. Monthly trends show that November and December have the highest sales, likely due to festivals and year-end offers.
Overall, the dashboard highlights both strong and weak areas, helping the company make better business decisions.

Conclusion

The company is profitable overall, but some categories and regions need improvement.This dashboard helps in tracking performance and making better business decisions.
